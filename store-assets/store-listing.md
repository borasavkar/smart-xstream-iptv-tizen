## Short Description (EN)
AI-powered IPTV player for Xtream Codes. Live TV, movies and series with 4K streaming, smart previews and a modern remote-friendly interface.

## Description (EN)
Smart Xtream AI IPTV Player turns your Samsung Smart TV into a powerful streaming hub for your own IPTV subscription (Xtream Codes API).

KEY FEATURES
• Multi-profile login with TV-keyboard-friendly forms (SmartThings supported)
• Live TV with instant channel zapping, EPG info and favorites
• Movies & series with categories, search, details, episodes and resume
• Quick Preview: rest on any poster to open a cinematic popup with an inline
  video preview, plot, cast, rating and language badges
• Modern player: YouTube-style controls, Netflix-style accelerated seeking,
  audio/subtitle/quality selection, styled subtitles
• Continue Watching and personalized recommendation rails
• 11 interface languages (Turkish, English, German, French, Spanish, Italian,
  Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Arabic)

NOTE: This app does not provide any content. A valid IPTV subscription from
your own provider (Xtream Codes login) is required. Use the built-in Demo
Mode to explore the interface without an account.

## Review Comments (test ekibi için — ÖNEMLİ)
This application is an IPTV player client and does NOT provide or sell any
content. For review/testing WITHOUT an IPTV account, please use the built-in
demo: on the profile screen press "Load Demo Data" ("Demo Verilerini Yükle").
Demo mode loads sample categories, movies and series with playable sample
streams so every feature (browsing, quick preview, player, favorites,
history) can be fully tested. Remote keys: arrows = navigate, OK = select,
Return = back. Hold ◀/▶ during VOD playback for accelerated seeking; rest on
a poster for ~2 seconds to open the Quick Preview popup.
